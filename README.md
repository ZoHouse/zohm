# Zo World

> **"This is the craziest human coordination layer we are building... serious emergence taking place."**

Zo World is a platform designed to facilitate high-trust human coordination through verifying real-world identity, reputation, and localized community governance. It bridges the digital and physical worlds, empowering "Citizens" to unlock specialized access to events, spaces, and opportunities based on their contributions and "Vibe".
